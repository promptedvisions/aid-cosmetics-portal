Regulatory HR Requirements - ISO 22716 & MOH

ISO 22716 GMP Requirements
1. Qualified Personnel

All personnel must be qualified by education, training, and experience

2. Training Program

Documented training for GMP, hygiene, job-specific tasks

3. Job Descriptions

Clear roles, responsibilities, and reporting structure

4. Hygiene Requirements

Health checks, personal hygiene, protective clothing

5. Training Records

Maintain records of all training activities and competency assessments

Israeli MOH Requirements
1. Responsible Person

Designated person for regulatory compliance (usually QA Manager)

2. Safety Officer

Required by law - can be external for small facilities

3. Hebrew Documentation

Key documents and labels must be in Hebrew

4. Product Release Authority

QA Manager must have authority to release products

5. Inspection Readiness

Personnel trained to handle MOH inspections

Initial Training

New employees must complete:

  • GMP introduction (4 hours)
  • Hygiene training (2 hours)
  • Job-specific SOPs (8-16 hours)
  • Safety orientation (2 hours)
Refresher Training

Annual requirements:

  • GMP update (2 hours)
  • New procedures review
  • Deviation case studies
  • Regulatory changes
Competency Assessment

Verification methods:

  • Written tests (80% pass)
  • Practical demonstrations
  • Observation checklists
  • Annual performance review
